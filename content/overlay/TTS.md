# TTS

Your message gets read by TTS for free. Voice and pop-cat are random.

Change voice with `%refreshvoice` (gamble).

Modifiers: `[fast]`, `[slow]`, `[high]`, `[low]`.
Example: `[fast] i am [slow] really [high] cool`

Sound effects: `(soundeffectname)` -- run `!sfx` for the list.
Example: `here, have some metal pipes. (metalpipes)`
