# Subscriptions & Bits

Support vanor and unlock perks.

## Subscriptions (Monthly)

|tier|perks|
|---|---|
|Tier 1|Sub badge, emotes, ad-free|
|Tier 2|Longer TTS (200 chars), priority TTS queue, Tier 1 perks|
|Tier 3|VIP status + [VIP commands]({{<relref "../commands.md#vip-overlay-commands" >}}), special chat color, Tier 2 perks|

## Bits (One-time)

Cheer with bits to highlight your message.

Bits skip TTS cooldowns. Large bit cheers trigger on-stream effects.
